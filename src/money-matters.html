Money Matters

A money skills programme (For clients diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder)

The programme aimed to teach clients who have been diagnosed with autism to identify and count money, understand the concept of change, and purchase items. These clients were from the St Andrew Autism Centre (Day Activity Centre Seng Kang).

A total of four classroom tasks were designed, and clients were taught individually how to complete the tasks before attempting them on their own. They were praised for correct performance and corrected immediately via physical, verbal, or gestural prompts when they made a mistake.

Money Matters was concluded as a programme that effectively increased clients’ money skills, allowing them to purchase items from stores appropriately. Learning resources were also shared with the Day Activity Centre so that more clients can benefit from them.
